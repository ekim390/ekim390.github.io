Welcome!

My name is Erin Minji Kim,

a Computer Engineer specializing in Devices & Distributed Systems & Software Design, and I strive to collaborate with others, develop new ideas, and explore the world.

"Let us make our future now, and let us make our dreams tomorrow’s reality.” – Malala Yousafzai"

I strive to follow my dreams while pursuing my career.

I am a 4th year Computer Engineering major with a minor in International Business pursuing her B.S. at The Georgia Institute of Technology. Although I was born in Hawaii, I lived a majority of my life in Georgia, surrounded by a rich cultural diversity. Friends and neighbors would share bits and pieces of their heritage and culture with me. I dreamed of exploring the world and seeing these stories with my own eyes. As I went through high school, I gained another dream: to pursue a career involving software and hardware. These series of events brought me to Georgia Tech to pursue Computer Engineering and even gain cultural/professional experience abroad at KAIST and Yonsei in South Korea.
